Questions

Common questions

How much time does our team need to commit?
Plan for key stakeholders to be available for: 90 minute AI Crash Session (Week 1), individual interviews (30 to 60 mins each, Week 1), pilot design workshops (Week 3), and final presentation (Week 4). Leadership decision makers should be involved throughout.
What if we're not sure we're ready?
That's what the discovery call is for. We'll assess your situation together and tell you honestly whether AI Activation makes sense for you right now. Sometimes the answer is "not yet" and that's okay.
What happens after the 30 days?
You have everything you need to execute independently. Your pilots are designed step by step, with clear success metrics and decision frameworks. If you want hands on support during implementation, we offer AI Acceleration for ongoing partnership.
How is this different from hiring a consulting firm?
Traditional consultants give you a strategy deck and leave. We work WITH your team, design pilots that are immediately executable, include champion training so your people can lead, and schedule a 30 day check in to see how you're progressing.
Can you work with our existing IT team or vendors?
Yes. We focus on strategy and conceptualization. Your IT team or vendors handle implementation. Our pilot designs are tool agnostic unless there's a clear best choice.
