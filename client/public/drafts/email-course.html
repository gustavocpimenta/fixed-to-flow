The Hidden Cost

AI is everywhere. Most people are using it wrong.

Not wrong in a technical sense. Wrong in a "this might actually make you worse at your job" sense.

AI tools are powerful. They give you short-term superpowers. But there's a hidden cost: when you offload too much thinking to AI, you stop exercising the mental muscles that make you valuable.

The pattern is clear: use it or lose it.

This course is about using AI in ways that make you stronger, not weaker. Building capability, not dependency.
