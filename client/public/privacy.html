Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

This policy explains how GUSTAVO PIMENTA UNIPESSOAL LDA ("Fixed to Flow", "we", "us") collects and uses information when you visit fixedtoflow.com.

What We Collect

Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. Your data is stored securely and processed by Resend (our email service provider) to deliver notifications to us.

Analytics (with your consent)

If you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type and browser
  • Approximate location (country/city level)
  • How you found us (referral source)

This data is anonymized and helps us improve the site. You can decline analytics cookies when prompted, and we will not track your visit.

Third-Party Services

We use these services to operate the site:

  • Google Analytics - Website analytics (Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Resend - Email delivery for contact form (Resend's Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel - Website hosting (Vercel's Privacy Policy)

Our site links to external platforms (Substack, LinkedIn, Medium, etc.) which have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those when you visit them.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact form on our website. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions - Retained while relevant to ongoing communication, then deleted
  • Analytics data - Retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default)

Cookies

We only use cookies for Google Analytics, and only after you give consent. No cookies are set if you decline. You can change your preference by clearing your browser's local storage and revisiting the site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.
