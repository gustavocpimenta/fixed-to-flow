Questions

Common questions

What happens in the "build together" session?
We start by identifying your biggest time drains. Then we build your first AI assistant together, live, on your screen. You leave with something immediately useful, not just notes to review later.
What if I don't know what AI tools to use?
That's part of what we figure out together. Based on your work and challenges, we'll identify which tools make sense for you. We're tool agnostic, we recommend what fits your situation.
How much time does this require?
The build together session is 90 minutes. After that, you implement at your own pace over 30 days. Most people spend 15 to 30 minutes per day during the implementation phase.
What's the difference between Basic and Extended?
Basic is for self starters who need a strong start then can run independently. Extended adds ongoing support: two extra check in calls, extended async support for faster responses, milestone check ins at 30 and 90 days, and a year of monthly Office Hours with other participants.
What if it doesn't work for me?
The qualification call exists to make sure we're a good fit before you invest. If we're not, we'll tell you and point you to something better suited. We only work with people we believe we can actually help.
