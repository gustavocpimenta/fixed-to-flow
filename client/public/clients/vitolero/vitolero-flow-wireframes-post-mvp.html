Post-MVP Roadmap

Phase 2 and beyond. Flows preserved as directional reference for the long game: recommendations from the taste profile, smoking diary with ratings, taste evolution, and camera-roll photo import.

May 2026 · Fixed to Flow

Not for discussion now

These flows are deliberately out of scope for the current MVP build. They remain on this page as long-game reference. The trajectory: where Vitolero goes after the MVP ships and validates the snap-and-share loop.

Read for context, not for action. Per the PRD §10, Phase 2 (recommendations from taste profile) ships weeks after MVP launch once Journal data reaches meaningful volume per user. Phase 3 expands pairing scenarios. Phase 4 (dedicated cigar social network) requires $500K-$1M funding.

All MVP work lives on the main flow wireframes page. Source of truth: the PRD §10.
