The Partnership

What you get

Strategic Sparring

  • 6 hours per month of 1:1 sessions
  • Flexible scheduling: 3x2hr, 6x1hr, or any combination
  • Direct WhatsApp access (weekdays)
  • Session summaries with key insights

What to expect

  • Help navigating complexity, not just strategy

Strategic Sessions (Add on)

When you hit a challenge that needs more than 1:1 thinking, bring your team. We design and facilitate a focused session around a specific problem. You leave with clarity and an action plan.

Up to 6 participants (you + your key people)
2 hours to half day (based on the challenge)
